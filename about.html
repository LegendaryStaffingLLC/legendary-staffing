About Legendary Staffing

Based in the heart of Las Vegas, Legendary Staffing is the premier partner for elite front-of-house solutions. From the Venetian Expo to the Mandalay Bay Convention Center, we provide the talent that defines the guest experience.

Staffing Guarantees

We operate on a 100% Reliability Guarantee. If a team member does not meet the "Legendary Standard" within the first 2 hours of a shift, they are replaced, and those hours are non-billable.

Bill Rates & Transparency

Our rates are all-inclusive of payroll taxes, workers' comp, and insurance. We provide transparent, flat-fee billing with no hidden "convention surcharges," allowing for precise budget forecasting.

Client MSA Summary

  • Payment Terms: Standard Net 30 from the final date of the event.
  • Cancellation: 50% of the total estimate is due if cancelled within 7 days of the event; 100% within 72 hours.
  • Liability: Legendary Staffing maintains $2M General Liability and $1M Workers' Compensation coverage.
  • Non-Solicitation: Clients agree not to directly hire Legendary talent for 12 months post-event without a buyout fee.
